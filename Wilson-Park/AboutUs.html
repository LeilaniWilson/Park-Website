Yellowstone national Park was established in 1872 and is located primarily in Wyoming. It is America's first national park. To this day yellowstone still remains the country's most popular national parks with around 4 million annual visitors. The whole park spans around 3,500 miles and extends into parts of Montana and Idaho, which makes it the largest national park in the United States.

50% of the worlds hydrothermal features are at Yellowstone National Park. Old Faithful is the parks most popular and recognized natural wonders in the United States. To learn more about Old Faithful click here.
